FILE-A · BIO A.A · 07-597

Hamilton, late hours. A compiler error and a cup of cold coffee. Somewhere in between, a story takes shape.

I'm a software developer finishing a Computer Systems Technology diploma at Mohawk College, graduated April 2026. I write Java, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and PHP. I build small worlds in Unity and Unreal Engine, edit film, and ship projects that started as ideas scribbled at 2 a.m.
